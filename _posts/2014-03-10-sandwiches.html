While I don't exactly have any legit cravings per se, I have been making these sandwiches that I think will be good because I'm hungry, and trying to be budget-conscious. So far, I really like my little lunch/dinner creations, but I've only made 2 so far. I believe I would eat these even if I weren't pregnant, but Chris isn't so sure. I'll post the recipes here, and try them again post-pregnancy just to see.

Under The Sea Delight

  • Bread
  • Seafood Salad
  • Guacamole
  • Black Olive Slices

First, slice your bread. I used sub bread. Smear the Guac on there like mayo. Then dump 1/4 lb of Seafood Salad. I like the Jewel deli kind. Lastly, sprinkle the seafood salad with black olive slices. Then eat. YUM!

Mermaid Fart

  • Onion Bagel
  • Cream Cheese
  • Sardines in Olive Oil

Toast your bagel. Smear the cream cheese on the bagel, I like the Onion & Chive kind. Then put your sardines on the sandwich. Put it together and eat!

I enjoy a gallon of orange juice with each sandwich. I hope you will too.
